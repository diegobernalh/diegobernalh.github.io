Website

a data-driven design firm in milan, italy, and new york city, accurat offers a variety of design services for its clients, creating high-end custom data visualizations and discovering correlations and trends in data pulled from its geospatial visual analysis platform. we want a super plain site using a minimal navigation system, bold gradients with the single monospace typeface akkurat mono. keeping the site completely static to ensure short download times. as a language we implemented jekyll to generate html pages and tachyons as a css framework.
