—DIGITAL ASSET MODEL

UX/UI & App Design — SNAM / ACR 2020—present

Design support for a complex data-driven interface used to monitor and manage large-scale infrastructure across a national energy network. The platform organizes real-time and historical data for each physical asset — stations, compressors, sensors — into a unified, user-friendly experience.

What it does:
Enables operators and maintenance planners to visualize and interact with a Digital Asset Model — a structured representation of every physical asset in the network, its status, relationships, and performance over time.

Design focus:
Structuring asset hierarchies for intuitive navigation. Developing clear visual indicators for status, alerts, and performance metrics. Ensuring consistency across components within the Asset Control Room ecosystem.

The challenge:
Serving multiple user types — from control room operators who need at-a-glance clarity to maintenance planners who need depth — without compromising either. Industrial data at this scale has no room for ambiguity.
