pepe pino wood glasses.

An effort to create a product that embrace individuality and uniqueness that can only be found in a natural setting. the desire to experiment with the design, materials and aesthetics in search of something that works. The focus of the brand is still that of their roots, innovation and creation. The manufacturing process and classical craftsmanship merge to create a unique art form. where completely handmade pieces are promoted. all our woods are made of recycled wood scraps and reforested , giving them a new utility, the timber handling is minimized in order to highlight the natural approach.
